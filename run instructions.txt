when at most files are changed:
[run from /gui-test]
cmake --build build_release
cmake --build build_debug
[or run from directory directly]
cmake --build .

when (re)creating the /build_[...]/ directory or changing CMakeLists.txt:
[run from /gui-test]
cmake -G "MinGW Makefiles" -B build_release -DCMAKE_BUILD_TYPE=Release .
cmake -G "MinGW Makefiles" -B build_debug -DCMAKE_BUILD_TYPE=Debug .

or for Ninja:
cmake -G Ninja -B build_release -DCMAKE_BUILD_TYPE=Release .
cmake -G Ninja -B build_debug -DCMAKE_BUILD_TYPE=Debug .
